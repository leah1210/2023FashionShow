Danielle Dean


I grew up in a small town about 30 minutes away from Stevenson. At a young age, I found my love for fashion, which led me to complete a fashion trade program in high school focusing on childrenswear. To this day my heart lies in the children’s wear world and everything that impacts it. In addition to children’s wear, I enjoy designing women’s formal wear and all the layers and details that go into the garment. But my passion doesn’t stop at designing; I love the business side of fashion as well.


The name for my collection is Pegasus and was highly inspired by how much children love to have fun with their clothing. I took a lot of my inspiration from a brand called Lola and the Boy and mixed it with modern children's clothing trends. This allowed me to step out of my comfort zone when it comes to designing. In order to match my designs to inspiration, I was able to mix different fabrics ranging from jersey knits to chiffon. My goal with my designs was to inspire kids to always be creative and express who they are at a young age through their clothing.
