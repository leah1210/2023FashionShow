Maryuri Membreno Navas


As a child, I was always drawing and loved art class. One day my grandmother handed me a fashion sketchbook, which made me realize how much I loved clothes. I would have fashion shows with my friends and drape sheets in all the ways I could think of to create dresses. I dressed myself from the moment I could make decisions and my parents gave me the freedom to do so even if I walked outside with mismatched socks. When I got to high school choosing a degree was difficult but the only thing that excited me was fashion design. Being a fashion design major comes with self-doubt and sleepless nights but there has not been a moment where I felt like I belonged anywhere else.


My evening wear collection, Opulence, is inspired by extravagance. The Opulence Collection includes flowy organza, silks, and shimmery lames. My goal was to use feminine shapes and eye-catching fabrics to make the wearer feel beautiful. I used couture techniques and high-end fabrics for my target customer who enjoys luxury items. They love to be the center of attention when walking into a room. I want anyone who wears my collection to feel confident and glamorous.
