Mina Grubb


I have always enjoyed fashion, especially the construction and sustainability aspects. When designing I take inspiration from nature and different locations (cities, towns, etc.). I primarily used natural fabrics with earthy and muted tones within my designs. I would describe my style as light, romantic, and sensual. Outside of fashion I enjoy trying out different mediums and media such as crocheting, painting, macrame, etc. As a designer I want to create clothing that people can enjoy and express themselves with while wearing.
