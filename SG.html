Shanel Gray


I’ve spent all my life in different areas in Maryland, ranging from DC to the Eastern Shore. I get a lot of inspiration from drag, the punk movement, and the black artists I’ve grown up loving. Outside of fashion design, I enjoy illustration, writing, and a little gaming. Honestly more than anything, I just want to be able to create things that the people I love can be proud of. But ya know a little fame wouldn’t hurt either.


The name for my collection is Systematic. My whole collection was made to represent some of the different societal issues that we see infecting day-to-day life. Knits, denim, duck cloth, and a whole bunch of fabric paint to brought it all together. I took a lot of inspiration from punk and grunge streetwear. Customer wise, I feel like my market lies in young, and most likely marginalized, folks. I like the idea of creating a sort of wearable protest.
