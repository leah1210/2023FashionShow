Tajiel Wesley


I was born and raised in Baltimore, Maryland with my 2 younger brothers and sister. I grew up a shy kid that enjoyed drawing, playing video games and spending time with family. I’ve always loved creating and spending time outdoors. I find inspiration in nature, basketball, and hip-hop. I came to Stevenson to pursue Graphic Design until I decided that it just wasn’t what I wanted to do. I enjoy fashion as a means to create my own reality and express my love for art.


As a tall darkskin man with a strong frame, people usually see me and assume that I play a sport. Although this is true for me, there is typically more than meets the eye. I wanted to parallel this notion with volcanoes and how often people may see dormant volcanoes and mistake them for mountains. They assume that since it has all the qualities of a mountain that it must be true. Like volcanoes, we can become overlooked for the brilliance that’s inside of us. However, when it comes out, it’s exciting and explosive like lava.
My collection, Volcanic Brilliance, I chose to design menswear to represent my experience as a black man on a daily basis. I used ornate lace fabric throughout my collection to symbolize the brilliance inside of us. Smooth tapered lines are used in silhouettes to imitate the natural shape of a volcano. My goal was for customers wearing my collection to feel seen for their own personal creativity.
