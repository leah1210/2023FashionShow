About Our Program 



Stevenson’s Fashion Design program develops the individual aesthetic of the designer through art and fashion design courses incorporating theory, technology and professional practice. Students gain an understanding of the fashion industry and history, consumer psychology, materials, and techniques of apparel construction. The coursework focuses primarily on studio work and other discipline-specific classes central to developing a design voice and vision as a fashion design professional.
